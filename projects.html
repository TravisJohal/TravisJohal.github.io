Here are some projects I’ve worked on:

Autonomous Rasberry Pi Car

For this project, a Raspberry Pi acts as the central brain, continuously reading distance measurements from onboard ultrasonic sensors to “see” obstacles in its path. Those sensor readings feed into a Python program that implements a simple obstacle-avoidance routine: when an object comes within a preset threshold, the code sends PWM signals through a motor-driver board to two DC motors, slowing, stopping, or steering the car away from hazards, all without human intervention.

Space Autocare Website

I worked in a team to create a website for Space AutoCare, a local car-detailing shop. We designed and built a site to showcase their full range of detailing services, integrate an intuitive online booking form, and deliver a fast, responsive experience across desktop and mobile devices.
